What Boberto Nation is

Boberto Nation is a youtube channel I created which focuses on providing anime music videos for people to watch. These anime music videos, or AMV's for short, consist of short clips from anime edited overtop some background music to match the beat or words in the song and allow viewers an idea of what an anime might be about without spoiling the plot.

Where it started

Originally, I had no plans to start a youtube channel, but as I was experementing with creating AMV's in my free time, I figured why not post them online as well. After achieving a moderate amount of sucess, I decided to spend some more effort into my youtube channel, making it into what it is today.

About Boberto Nation

This YouTube channel focuses on creating anime music videos with unique takes and impressions in each one. As with any content derived from another source, spoilers may be contained within any created video, and it is therefore recommended that a viewer first watches the anime a video is based on before proceeding to the video itself. Boberto Nation does not own any rights or claims to the anime or music used in its videos, the source material will always be listed in the description.
